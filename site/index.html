// Government value

A simpler path to integrated government services.

By working together, participating Digital Public Goods make it easier for governments to adopt, implement and scale integrated solutions, with the value of the whole stack greater than any single platform alone.

One coordinated entry point to integrated solutions

A single interface presenting the full collaborative offer, where governments engage with complementary Digital Public Goods as one coherent stack, not as separate platforms to evaluate independently.

A clear pathway from discovery through to implementation

Step-by-step guidance covering exploration, evaluation, procurement and delivery, with shared proposal templates, reference architectures and engagement models reducing complexity at every stage.

A trusted ecosystem of implementation partners

A curated directory of system integrators with demonstrated capability across multiple Digital Public Goods, so governments can identify and engage partners who know how to deliver integrated solutions.

// Our commitments

Shared commitments, upheld by every participating DPG.

These are the commitments each participating Digital Public Good makes to governments and to one another, the shared discipline that lets a group of independent platforms present a single, coherent offer.

01

Country ownership and sovereignty

Governments retain full control over their systems, data, and how solutions are implemented and evolve over time.

02

Driven by service outcomes

Success is the real-world impact on citizens, with strong safeguards for privacy and security built in.

03

Built for interoperability

Openly documented, non-proprietary interfaces that combine into integrated, end-to-end public services without lock-in.

04

Security by design

Systems, data and integrations are designed and maintained to strong security practices, upholding trust across the ecosystem.

05

Sustainable by design

Solutions are affordable to run, maintainable by a competitive ecosystem, and free of dependency on any single vendor.

06

Complementarity over competition

Participating Digital Public Goods are positioned to work together as components of one integrated service stack.
